Short Review

Abhiyum Naanum

Abhiyum Naanum is a stroy which portrays the relationship between a father and a daughter named Abhi. Prakash Raj stars in the role as the father and Trisha acts as the daughter. This story beautifully pictures their bond and the struggles in their relationship as she grows up.

Un Samayal Arayil

Kalidasa (Prakash Raj) works in the state archaeological department and is a food lover, he is 45 years old and remains unmarried. His only companion is his cook, Krishna (Thambi Ramaiah ).
Naveen (Tejus) is Kalidasa's nephew who comes to stay with him while looking for a job. Kalidasa has a normal life until he gets a misdialed phone call from Gowri (Sneha), a dubbing artiste living with her friend Meghana

Saivam

Saivam (Vegetarianism) is a 2014 Tamil drama film. The film features Nassar in the lead role and child actress Sara Arjun plays another pivotal role. The girl hides her grandfather's rooster to save it from being sacrificed for good luck.

Rang De Basanti

Rang De Basanti is a 2006 Indian Hindi-language drama film. After a group of friends graduate from Delhi University, they listlessly haunt their old campus, until a British filmmaker (Alice Patten) casts them in a film she's making about freedom fighters under British rule.
Although the group is largely apolitical, the tragic death of a friend owing to local government corruption awakens their patriotism. Inspired by the freedom fighters they represent in the film, the friends collectively decide to avenge the killing

Taare Zameen Par

Taare Zameen Par is a 2007 Indian Hindi-language drama. Ishaan, a student who has dyslexia, cannot seem to get anything right at his boarding school.
Soon, a new unconventional art teacher, Ram Shankar Nikumbh, helps him discover his hidden potential.

Hundred Foot Journey

Hassan Kadam (Manish Dayal) is an extraordinarily talented and largely self-taught culinary novice. When he and his family are displaced from their native India and settle in a quaint French village, they decide to open an Indian eatery.
However, Madame Mallory (Helen Mirren), the proprietress of an acclaimed restaurant just 100 feet away, strongly objects. War erupts between the two establishments, until Mallory recognizes Kadam's impressive epicurean gifts and takes him under her wing.

English Vinglish

English Vinglish is a 2012 Indian comedy-drama film. A tradition-minded Indian housewife (Sridevi) enrolls in an accelerated English-language course after she finds herself unable to place a simple order in an American restaurant.

Lion

Lion (pronounced Leon) is a 2016 biographical drama film. Lion tells the true story of how Brierley, 25 years after being separated from his family in Burhanpur, sets out to find them.
